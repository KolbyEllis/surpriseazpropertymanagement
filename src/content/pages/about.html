Our Pricing is very competitive

We maintain a straightforward fee structure. Our leasing fee covers the costs associated with finding a tenant, specifically advertising, and agent agent comissions. Following occupancy, our management fee is a percentage of the rent collected each month.

There are no other fees for lease renewal, admin fees, set up fees, advertising, etc. It’s very simple, nothing up front and once leased the leasing fee comes from the first month of rent. When all the misc. fees others charge are added into the equation, we are very competitively priced. You can find property managers that will “manage” your property for $50 per month but cheap is not usually your best choice.

Property management is ingrained in our family's heritage

In business since 1989

My father owned about 70 rental properties and as a child I was always around rental homes. My rental background was born from helping him with minor maintenance issues, and then to full blown rent collection for his 70 properties at the age of 16. Once I graduated from Missouri Southern State University, I immediately started my own company in Missouri in 1989. For most of the past 20 years I have owned 120 rental houses myself and managed another 70+ for other clients. I have also developed several subdivisions, strip malls, and built several hundred homes (some of which I kept as rentals for myself). Rental homes and property management is all I've known all my working life and I am good at it. My wife Cindi joined me in the business 12 years ago after our youngest started school. Cindi has a remarkable personality and does very well with marketing to and taking care of the tenants and property management clients.
